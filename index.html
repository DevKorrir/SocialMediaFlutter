Welcome to Group Ten Herbal Haven

Discover the finest herbal teas, harvested with care and sourced from local farmers who take pride in sustainable practices. Our farmers use organic fertilizers, such as compost and animal waste, ensuring that no harmful chemicals are introduced to the soil. This commitment to natural farming not only preserves the environment but also enhances the flavor and purity of our teas. Each leaf is carefully handpicked at its peak, guaranteeing only the best quality is selected. Through meticulous processing, we craft our teas to retain their natural goodness, delivering a rich, authentic taste with every sip. Experience wellness in every cup, from farm to teapot.

About The Herbal Haven

MISSION

To provide our customers with the highest quality herbal teas, sustainably sourced from local farmers, while promoting health, wellness, and environmental stewardship. We are dedicated to offering products that are natural, pure, and crafted with care, ensuring each cup delivers a taste of nature's finest ingredients

VISION

To become the leading herbal tea brand in East Africa, known for our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. We envision a future where our teas are enjoyed by people from all walks of life, and our business is a force for good in the communities we serve.

Core Values

Our core values are the foundation of our business and guide our decisions every day.

  • Quality: From farm to cup, we ensure the highest standards in every step of our production process.
  • Sustainability: We believe in protecting the earth through organic farming and sustainable sourcing practices.
  • Community: We support local farmers by working directly with them and ensuring fair trade practices
  • Integrity: We maintain transparency and honesty in everything we do, ensuring trust in our products and practices.
